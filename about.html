About Us

Welcome to the cocktail life guide. I am here to help you find, research,
and create your favorite cocktails. I have information on the 50 most
popular cocktails out there and I would like to share them to you.
Time and time again I have tried delicious cocktails but had no idea
what I was tasting. By the time I thought to take a picture of the
cocktail to remember later, I have already finished the drink. To solve
this dilemma I bring you my cocktail guide. Browse through each
cocktail's picture if you don't remember the name of the cocktail.
If you do please search for your cocktail and learn to make it at the
comfort of your home.
